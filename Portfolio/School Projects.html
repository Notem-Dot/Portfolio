Computing Coursework 2024

For our coursework, our group wanted to focus on the area of Public Speaking as well as Oral Practice/Examinations in schools. Our solution tracks the time between instances where the public speaker uses filler words such as "Uhm" or "Uh". The data would then be used to create a point on a graph with the X-Axis being Standard Deviation and Y-Axis being Average Time. This allows the user to have a visual representation of how they are improving.
