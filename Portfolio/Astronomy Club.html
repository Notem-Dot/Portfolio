Astro Club Events

NASA Hyperwall Talks

During the Hyperwall talks, I was able to meet scientists such as Steven D Vance, Weile Wang, Pawan Gupta, Natasha Sadoff, Jeremy Werdell and Jack Kaye. Their presentations were very insightful and gave me the oppportunity to ask my questions to experts in their fields of study.

JAPANESE VISITORS TO SST(25 AUG 2023)

On 25 Aug 2023, my exco and I created a short presentation about astro club and applied learning within SST to present to the japanese visitors that were teachers. During the presentation, a translator was there to translate everything I said to Japanese in real time.

Tanjung Leman Overseas Trip

Astro Club held an overseas learning journey during the March Holidays of 2023. During which, we partook in various activities such as creating a model to show how solar eclipses occur as well as intercepting the radio waves from satellites flying overhead.

Lunar Eclipse Overnight Camp (23 Oct 2023)

During the camp, we had various activities relating to Radio Astronomy and invited Mr. Hugh Mason from SARTS to give a lecture on how antennas work, high frequency radio transmissions and receptions as well as space weather phenomenon.

Outreach At Jurong Green CC

We were part of an astronomy outreach program at Jurong Green CC. Unfortunately midway during the event, it started to rain and the program had to be cancelled. We had to keep up our equipment and unplug all electrical devices as a safety precaustion. I then used my umbrella to ferry members of the public that were trapped by the rain at our booth to the nearby buildings so they could go home.

Outreach At Casa Clementi

We were part of another astronomy outreach program at Casa Clementi. Luckily, the weather was clear this time and everything went smoothly.
